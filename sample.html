Adult Climbing Sessions for The Hive Vancouver

Who: Adults 18 and up!
Cost: Regular pricing in effect

The Hive is asking users / members to understand access to the facilities is limited, so please respect the following items:

- Users must practice social distance at all times while in our facilities with anyone not part of their immediate household
- Users must wear a well-fitting (covering nose and mouth) mask consisting of multiple layers at all times while in the facility; buffs, gaiters, bandanas, and masks with vents are no longer acceptable as per government health regulations
- Users agree not to book more than 4 sessions per week (Monday - Sunday) per location
- Users will cancel bookings they cannot attend via the RGPro Connect app or by calling the appropriate facility (emails/voicemails not accepted). Failure to do so will result in remaining sessions being cancelled without notice.
- Users who have been outside Canada in the last 14 days, been exposed to anyone confirmed with, or displayed any known symptoms of COVID-19 are strictly prohibited use of Hive facilities at this time.
- Users must wash their hands as regularly as possible; Customer\'s hands must be washed upon entry
- All customers must practice social distancing (2m) with everyone in the facility at all times
- All Waivers must be updated prior to your first visit; there may be significant delays during check-in if waivers are not completed online
- We are ONLY permitting the use of liquid chalk until further notice; options available for purchase if necessary
- Respect your booking time; Please arrive on time! You will be asked to start packing up 15 minutes prior to the end of the booking
- You must exit the facility by the end of your booking time
- Arrive changed and ready to climb; access to changing areas and storage will be limited or restricted based on facility
- Only bring to the gym what is necessary (shoes, water bottle)
- Areas in the facility have strict capacity limits, please pay attention to signage and respect those limits
- Wipe down any / all fitness or training equipment used with supplied disinfectant
- Facility will ONLY be accessible via confirmed bookings

Some of our usual services are impacted due to our new COVID-19 regulations. Thank you for your understanding.

- Reduced locker availability
- No towel / linen service
- Reduced change facilities

- Closure of water fountains (opportunities for bottle refill available)

How can you prove a mask is 3 layers?
We can\'t \xe2\x80\x93 we are not looking to police masks at this level, we are looking to eliminate inadequate mask substitutes, and educate the community on how to protect themselves and others. As climbers, we are each individually capable of and responsible for managing risk to the best of our ability \xe2\x80\x93 as you should only use the best safety gear when climbing, so we should use the best mask options as well.

What about 2 layer masks?
We aren\'t going to deny access to those with a 2 layer mask, but we recommend upgrading to a 3 layer mask as soon as you are able.

Can I buy a new 3-layer mask at The Hive?
Yes \xe2\x80\x93 we will have 3-layer masks for sale (likely at the same or very similar price as the previous 2 layer masks)

What\'s wrong with my buff/bandana/vented mask, exactly?
These options don\'t provide enough of a barrier, as per the recent announcements of increased risk from aerosolized virus spread by coughs and sneezes -- the open vent, loose weave, or single-layered nature of these masks just don\'t act as enough of a barrier for those micro-droplets.
Supporting documentation:
  • https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/three-layer-mask-tam-1.5787946
  • https://www.canada.ca/en/public-health/services/diseases/2019-novel-coronavirus-infection/prevention-risks/about-non-medical-masks-face-coverings.html#a1
  • http://www.phn-rsp.ca/sac-covid-ccs/wearing-masks-community-eng.php
Why is gym capacity limited?
Limiting gym access helps reduce crowds and ensure social distancing in an effort to lower the risk of virus transmission at the gym. We will continue to increase our capacity as we feel is responsible and is allowed within the provincial guidelines.

Why are you limiting the number of bookings I can make to 4 sessions per location per week?
During the summer months we relaxed our policy on booking limits, but recent demand indicates that this is no longer feasible. Our community is large and we are trying to create a policy that is fair to as many folks as possible.. We will monitor the impact of this new limit, and reserve the right to make changes.

This newly enforced limit affects me, how can I climb more often?
If you are someone who likes to train more than 4 times per week, we hope that you will take advantage of the Ultimate Membership perks (extended to all members until 31st March, 2021) by exploring one or all of our other Hive locations. - remember, the cap of 4 sessions per week is per location.

Why is your \xe2\x80\x98no show\xe2\x80\x99 policy so strict?
With a limited capacity in place we understand how valuable each climbing slot has become. Unfortunately, since we reopened we have seen an increase in the number of \xe2\x80\x98no shows\xe2\x80\x99, creating sometimes as many as 9 unused spots per session - and it would be unfair to allow this to continue. We are now introducing much stricter penalties for \xe2\x80\x98no shows\xe2\x80\x99 to ensure that we maximize all available space during each session, and give as many people as possible their time in the gym!

My plans have changed unexpectedly, how can I avoid \xe2\x80\x98no show\xe2\x80\x99 penalties?
To avoid being considered a \xe2\x80\x98no show\xe2\x80\x99 simply cancel your bookings via the RGPro Connect App, online, or by phone before your session begins. Download the App for iOS or Android.
We understand that sometimes life happens, so if you are delayed for a valid reason please call the front desk (no later than 30mins into your session), and let us know that we should still expect you.

Will \xe2\x80\x98Member Only Hours\xe2\x80\x99 be returning to the Hive Vancouver this peak season?
We have heard \xe2\x80\x98Member Only Hours\xe2\x80\x99 suggested by our members and although we are not ruling this out, currently we do not have plans to reinstate them at the Hive Vancouver this season. The reason for this is that more than 80% of our total bookings and close to 100% of our peak bookings (5-9PM weekdays) are currently made by members, so the impact would be minimal compared to other measures.

How do I bring my kids into the facility to rock climb?
In order to reserve space for youth climbers (ages 17 & under) please call the appropriate facility for availability and further information. Reservations for youth drop-ins can only be made over the phone at this time.

Can a friend take my booking spot?
Reservations are non-transferable.

If I cancel a booking, can I re-sign up?
You may book into a time slot right up until the start of that particular booking as long as space is available.

Can I book more than one person for each slot?
You may book up to two participants in your booking

Why are you only permitting liquid chalk use?
We are not permitting dry chalk in an effort to reduce the airborne chalk in our facilities. This particulate matter causes irritation to eyes and respiratory tract which may result in more frequent touching of eyes, nose and face, as well as coughing and sneezing.

Can I purchase more punches if my punches are running out?
Memberships and Punches are available for purchase online and in-gym.

Can I use my Member Guest Pass?
You are free to use your Member Guest Pass, but both you and your guest must be booked into the same time slot in order to ensure there is space available.

Can I share punch with a friend?
Punches may be shared as normal between users but both users must be booked into the same time slot in order to ensure there is space available.

I am not a member, can I sign up?
You may now purchase any membership online or in-gym.

How long do we have to book into time slots?
All Hive locations will continue to operate with a reservation system until further notice. Get the most up-to-date information by checking our website or following us on social media.
